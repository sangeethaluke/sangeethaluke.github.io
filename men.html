Nautica Voyage

Nautica Voyage is a fantastic fresh fragrance proven by time as likable and versatile.
Great Maurice Roucel created it in 2006.
It smells familiar because of the incredible popularity of fresh cucumber/melon calone fragrances
in the 90s and 00s, but also fashionable because of the renewed interest in fresh watery fragrances in recent years.

Gucci Guilty Eau de Parfum

Gucci Guilty Eau de Parfum by Gucci is a Amber Floral fragrance for men.
Gucci Guilty Eau de Parfum was launched in 2019.
Top notes are Pink Pepper, Mandarin Orange and Bergamot;
middle notes are Lilac, Violet, Geranium and Rose; base notes are Patchouli and Amber.
